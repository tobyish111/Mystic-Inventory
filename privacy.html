Mystic Inventory — Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 23, 2025

Mystic Inventory is an iOS app that helps you scan, organize, and analyze your Magic: The Gathering collection. We take privacy seriously: the app is designed so your collection data stays on your device, and we do not collect personal information.

Summary: No personal data collection

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII). No accounts, emails, or names are required to use the app.

  • On-device data: Your collections and cards are stored locally on your device using Apple frameworks.
  • No analytics: The app does not include analytics SDKs.
  • No ad tracking: The app does not use advertising identifiers.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Local storage: Collection and card data you create is saved locally on your device and is under your control.
  • Network requests: When you search or scan, the app queries third‑party card databases to retrieve card details and images.
  • Caching: Some responses are cached locally to improve speed and reduce network usage.

Third‑Party Services

Mystic Inventory connects to reputable public card databases to fetch rules text, images, and combo data:

  • Scryfall API — card data & images. See their privacy policy.
  • Commander Spellbook — EDH combo references (read‑only). Community resource without personal data collection.

Network requests may include the card names or identifiers you search for, which are not personal data.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Choices & Controls

  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.
  • If you wish to clear cached responses, reinstalling the app resets local caches.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if we introduce optional features). Any updates will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about privacy, please open an issue on our public tracker: GitHub Issues — Mystic Inventory.
